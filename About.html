What is Tech-Deeksha?

‘TechDeeksha’ where Deeksha is an initiation by the guru i.e. “when a teacher shows you the way and you follow that path”. TechDeeksha is an organization started by GNIT alumni in Nov’ 2019 to provide free technical courses to students. At TechDeeksha we believe that our future will be shaped by our core values: academic freedom, Integrity and Respect for the individual.

Mission

TechDeeksha aspires to act as a bridge for the students to smoothly transition into the professional world of IT sector. We will ensure that our students develop the essential technical and communication skills through our varied range of courses and competencies essential for success and leadership in the professional world. We work so that each student can access quality education which can help them to overcome the barrier they face while achieving their Goals. We welcome everyone to visit our site and explore as much as you want because Tech-Deeksha is a non-profit educational platform. Our aim here is to guide students, to help them to boost their knowledge in different fields. We also intend to prepare aspirants (fresher to experience) to overcome the hurdles they face in Interviews.

Vision

At TechDeeksha, our excellent and highly qualified IT professionals will mentor the students and provide theoretical and practical knowledge on varied range of technologies: MS SQL, MY SQL, HTML, CSS, JS, JQUERY, JAVA, PYTHON We will provide demo interview sessions and CV screening to every fresher to ease them into the IT sector and instill confidence into them.
